7. Commissions and Payouts

7.1 Commission Structure

Creators earn commissions on confirmed Bookings according to our tiered commission structure:

  • Starter Tier (40%): 1+ confirmed booking
  • Rising Tier (45%): 5+ bookings or $10,000+ total booking value
  • Established Tier (50%): 15+ bookings or $50,000+ total booking value
  • Elite Tier (55%): 50+ bookings or $150,000+ total booking value

Commission percentages are calculated as a share of JUNO's platform fee on each Booking.

7.2 Payout Terms

Creator commissions are paid according to the following terms:

  • Commissions are calculated after the Traveller completes their trip
  • Payouts are processed within 30 days of trip completion
  • Minimum payout threshold may apply

  • Creators are responsible for any applicable taxes on their earnings
