Mozambique Island Escape

Duration 5 Days / 4 Nights From $6,500 pp Region Bazaruto Archipelago
Curated by Nia The Light

The Journey

Mozambique offers something increasingly rare: pristine, uncrowded beaches in a setting of genuine luxury. The Bazaruto Archipelago, a chain of islands off the southern coast, feels like the edge of the world. Turquoise waters, endless sandbanks, and a marine national park teeming with life.

This itinerary centers on Anantara Bazaruto, an island resort that delivers on every front: beautiful villas, exceptional dining, and activities that range from blissful relaxation to ocean adventures. It's the kind of destination where days blur into each other in the best possible way.

Day by Day

Day 1

Arrival in Paradise

Fly from Johannesburg or Vilanculos to Bazaruto Island. The helicopter transfer from the mainland is an experience in itself, with aerial views of the archipelago's stunning colors. Arrive at Anantara Bazaruto and settle into your beach villa. The afternoon is yours to explore the property or simply collapse onto the beach. Sundowners on the sand, followed by dinner at the main restaurant.

Day 2

Ocean Adventures

After breakfast, choose your adventure: snorkeling in the marine reserve, a dhow sailing excursion, or deep-sea fishing in waters known for marlin and sailfish. The afternoon brings spa time or pool lounging. As the sun sets, join the beach dinner setup for a private meal on the sand with the Indian Ocean as your backdrop.

Day 3

Island Exploration

Take a guided walk to discover the island's interior, including freshwater lakes and local villages. The contrast between the lush interior and the endless beaches is striking. Afternoon at leisure before a sunset horse ride along the beach. Dinner at Tartaruga, the resort's seafood restaurant built over the water.

Day 4

Sandbank Picnic

The signature experience: a boat trip to a private sandbank in the middle of the ocean. Your guide sets up a gourmet picnic on what feels like the most remote beach on Earth. Snorkel the surrounding waters, spot dolphins, and spend hours in complete isolation. Return to the resort for your final sunset and a farewell dinner.

Day 5

Departure

A leisurely final breakfast before your helicopter transfer back to the mainland. Connect to flights home via Johannesburg, or extend your journey to Cape Town or a safari destination.
