Bazaruto, Mozambique

Island Escape

The Journey

Mozambique offers something increasingly rare: pristine, uncrowded beaches in a setting of genuine luxury. The Bazaruto Archipelago, a chain of islands off the southern coast, feels like the edge of the world. Turquoise waters, endless sandbanks, and a marine national park teeming with life.

I designed this escape for anyone who needs to truly unplug. Anantara Bazaruto delivers on every front: beautiful villas, exceptional dining, and activities that range from blissful relaxation to ocean adventures. Days blur into each other in the best possible way.
