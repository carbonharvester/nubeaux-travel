Kenya: Kilifi to Lamu

Duration 7 Days / 6 Nights From $4,500 pp Region Kenyan Coast
Curated by Nia The Light

The Journey

Kenya's coast is a world apart from its famous safari lands. This itinerary takes you along the Swahili coast, from the creative enclave of Kilifi to the historic streets of Lamu. It's a journey through centuries of culture, where African, Arab, and Portuguese influences blend into something uniquely beautiful.

This is slow travel at its best: dhow boats, ancient stone towns, and beaches that feel genuinely undiscovered. The Kenyan coast rewards those who take time to absorb its rhythms.

Day by Day

Day 1

Arrival in Mombasa, Transfer to Kilifi

Arrive at Moi International Airport in Mombasa. Transfer north along the coast to Kilifi, a town that's become a gathering place for creatives, entrepreneurs, and wellness seekers. Check into Tarangau Retreat, a serene property on the creek. Settle in and enjoy your first sunset over the water.

Day 2-3

Kilifi Days

Two days to explore Kilifi's unique vibe. Visit local artisan workshops and the thriving caf√© scene. Take a dhow trip up Kilifi Creek to see traditional boatbuilding. Spend time at the beach, book a yoga session, or simply read by the pool. Evenings offer excellent dining at waterfront restaurants with fresh seafood and international influences.

Day 4

Kilifi to Lamu

Morning at leisure before transferring to the airstrip for your flight to Lamu. The short flight offers stunning views of the coastline. Land on Manda Island and take a boat across to Lamu Old Town. Check into The Dhow House, a private beachfront property. Explore the narrow streets of the old town before dinner.

Day 5-6

Lamu Exploration

Two days in one of Africa's most atmospheric places. Lamu Old Town is a UNESCO World Heritage site where donkeys still outnumber cars and Swahili culture thrives. Visit the Lamu Museum, the ancient Riyadha Mosque, and the traditional dhow workshops. Take a sunset dhow cruise around the archipelago. Walk the beaches of Shela village. Dine on rooftop terraces overlooking the waterfront.

Day 7

Departure

Final morning in Lamu. Perhaps an early swim or a last walk through the old town. Boat to Manda for your flight to Mombasa or Nairobi, connecting to international flights or extending to safari in the Masai Mara.
