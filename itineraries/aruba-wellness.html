Aruba Wellness Retreat

The Journey

Aruba is unlike any other Caribbean island. Where turquoise waters meet desert landscapes, you'll find a unique backdrop for rest, renewal, and reconnection with yourself. This is the Caribbean stripped of pretense: endless sunshine, warm trade winds, and a pace of life that reminds you to breathe.

This wellness-focused retreat balances active exploration with intentional relaxation. Morning yoga on the beach, afternoon adventures through Arikok National Park, sunset dinners overlooking Eagle Beach. Every moment designed to restore.

Day by Day

Day 1

Arrival & Settle In

Arrive at Queen Beatrix International Airport and transfer to your oceanfront resort. Check into your suite with views of the Caribbean. Afternoon at leisure to acclimate: pool, beach, or spa. Welcome dinner at sunset featuring local Caribbean fusion cuisine.

Day 2

Beach Wellness Day

Begin with sunrise yoga on Eagle Beach, consistently rated among the world's best beaches. After a healthy breakfast, enjoy a morning of water activities: paddleboarding, snorkeling, or simply floating in the calm, crystal-clear waters. Afternoon spa treatment featuring aloe vera, Aruba's signature ingredient. Evening free to explore Oranjestad's waterfront dining.

Day 3

Desert & Natural Pools

Full day exploring Aruba's surprising interior. Morning jeep tour through Arikok National Park, discovering dramatic rock formations, ancient Arawak cave paintings, and the island's diverse wildlife. Lunch at a local kunuku (countryside farm). Afternoon at the Natural Pool, swimming in this hidden gem surrounded by volcanic rock. Return for sunset and dinner at your resort.

Day 4

Soul Restoration

A day designed for deep relaxation. Morning meditation session followed by a leisurely breakfast. Late morning full-body treatment at the spa. Afternoon at Palm Beach with optional activities: kite watching, beach walking, or reading in the shade of a palapa. Farewell dinner at a beachside restaurant with your toes in the sand.

Day 5

Departure

Final morning yoga session at sunrise. Leisurely breakfast before airport transfer. Depart renewed, carrying the island's warmth with you.
