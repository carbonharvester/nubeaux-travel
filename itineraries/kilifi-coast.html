Kilifi, Kenya

Coastal Retreat

The Journey

Kilifi represents Kenya's coast at its most authentic. This small town on the Indian Ocean has become a magnet for creatives, artists, and those seeking a slower pace. Unlike the busier beach resorts, Kilifi offers genuine Swahili culture alongside pristine beaches and excellent cuisine.

This itinerary immerses you in coastal Kenyan life: boutique stays overlooking the creek, fresh seafood, water sports, and the laid-back energy that makes this corner of Africa so special.

Day by Day

Day 1

Arrival in Kilifi

Transfer from Mombasa airport to your boutique hotel on Kilifi Creek. Check in and settle into the coastal rhythm. Afternoon free to swim, kayak, or simply relax by the pool. Sundowners overlooking the creek as the sun sets over the water. Dinner at the hotel featuring fresh Swahili cuisine.

Hotel Check-in Pool Time Sundowners
Day 2

Coastal Exploration

Morning boat trip along Kilifi Creek, visiting local fishing villages and learning about traditional boat-building. Lunch at a beachside restaurant in town. Afternoon free for optional activities: snorkeling at the marine park, paddleboarding, or exploring the local arts scene. Evening dinner at one of Kilifi's excellent restaurants known for blending Swahili and international flavors.

Boat Trip Snorkeling Paddleboarding Local Cuisine
Day 3

Departure

Leisurely morning with breakfast overlooking the creek. Time for a final swim or massage before transfer to Mombasa airport, or continue your journey to Lamu Island for a complete Kenyan coast experience.

Creek Breakfast Spa Treatment Airport Transfer
