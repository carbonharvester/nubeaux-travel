Namibia Safari

Duration 4 Days / 3 Nights From $4,500 pp Region Etosha & Onguma

The Journey

A concentrated Namibian safari experience at Onguma Game Reserve, bordering Etosha National Park. Three days of world-class game viewing, design-forward accommodation, and the otherworldly beauty of the Namibian landscape.

This is safari done right: intimate lodges, expert guides, and endless horizons. Perfect for adding to a longer Southern Africa journey or as an immersive standalone experience.

Day by Day

Day 1

Arrival at Onguma

Arrive at Onguma's private airstrip or transfer from Windhoek. Check into Onguma Camp Kala in time for lunch. Settle into your suite before an afternoon game drive into Etosha National Park. Sundowners overlooking the pan, followed by dinner under the stars at the lodge.

Lodge Check-in Game Drive Sundowners
Day 2

Full Safari Day

Early morning game drive into Etosha to catch the best wildlife activity. Return to camp for brunch and pool time during the midday heat. Afternoon drive explores different areas of the park, maximizing your chances of seeing lion, elephant, giraffe, rhino, and countless antelope. Optional night drive on Onguma's private reserve to spot nocturnal wildlife.

Morning Game Drive Pool & Brunch Afternoon Safari Night Drive
Day 3

Departure

Final morning game drive on Onguma's private reserve, where walking safaris are also possible. Brunch at the lodge before transfer to the airstrip for your onward flight, or extend your journey to Sossusvlei, the Skeleton Coast, or Johannesburg.

Walking Safari Farewell Brunch Airport Transfer
