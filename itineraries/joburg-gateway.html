Johannesburg Gateway

4 Days / 3 Nights From $2,800 pp Johannesburg
Curated by Nia The Light

The Journey

Johannesburg is Africa's most dynamic city, and it deserves more than just a transit stop. This itinerary gives you a proper introduction to Joburg's art scene, culinary creativity, and complex history. It's the perfect start or end to a Southern African safari, or a destination in its own right.

From world-class museums to street art, craft breweries to fine dining, Johannesburg rewards the curious traveller with experiences that challenge and delight in equal measure.

Day by Day

Day 1

Arrival & Rosebank

Arrive at OR Tambo International and transfer to your hotel in Rosebank. Depending on arrival time, explore the Rosebank neighborhood: its galleries, boutiques, and the famous Rosebank Sunday Market (if timing allows). Evening dinner at The Butcher Shop & Grill or Marble for contemporary South African cuisine.

Day 2

Art & History

Morning visit to the Apartheid Museum for a powerful introduction to South Africa's recent history. Lunch in Maboneng, the city's creative precinct, then explore its street art, galleries, and design shops. Afternoon at the Zeitz Museum of Contemporary Art Africa (if combining with Cape Town) or the Johannesburg Art Gallery. Dinner at The Marabi Club for jazz and cocktails.

Day 3

Soweto & Township Culture

Full-day guided tour of Soweto, South Africa's most famous township. Visit the Hector Pieterson Memorial, Mandela House Museum, and experience the vibrant street life. Lunch at a local shisa nyama (braai spot). Return via the Nelson Mandela Bridge for sunset views over the city. Final dinner at The Westcliff for hillside views and excellent cuisine.

Day 4

Departure

Morning at leisure for last-minute shopping at Neighbourgoods Market (Saturday) or 44 Stanley. Transfer to airport for onward journey to safari, Cape Town, or home.
