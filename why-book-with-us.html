The Problem

What you miss when you book through an OTA

Online travel agencies charge hotels over 20% in fees. To recoup costs, hotels deprioritise OTA bookings for upgrades, perks, and service.

No upgrade priority

OTA bookings are last in line for room upgrades and preferred room assignments.

Call centre support

When something goes wrong, you speak to a rotating cast of agents who don't know you.

No exclusive perks

No complimentary breakfast. No property credits. No VIP treatment.

Points & Rewards

You still earn your credit card points.

A common misconception: booking through a travel advisor means losing credit card rewards. That's not true.

You earn the same points on your card whether you book through us or directly. And at partner hotels, you still earn loyalty program points too.

"Save your points for flights, where they typically provide the best value. Let us handle hotels, where our perks add real value on top of points."
