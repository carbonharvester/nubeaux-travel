What you do. What we do.

Clear responsibilities. No overlap. No confusion.

You do

  • Share trips you've actually taken
  • Provide details about your stays
  • Share your itinerary link
  • Receive commission on bookings

You don't do

  • Handle enquiries or questions
  • Quote prices or negotiate
  • Process payments or bookings
  • Provide traveller support

How creators earn

Creators earn 40% of the total commission generated on confirmed bookings.

Higher tiers unlock as your bookings grow. No admin. No calls. No customer support. Just earnings from trips you've already taken.
