Frequently Asked Questions

For Travellers

Booking with JUNO

Browse trips from creators, find one you love, and click "Request Quote." We'll check availability for your dates, put together pricing, and send you a detailed quote within 24-48 hours.

Once you're happy with the quote, you can confirm your booking and we handle everything from there.

Absolutely. Every itinerary is a starting point. You can:

  • Adjust travel dates
  • Change trip length (add or remove days)
  • Upgrade or change accommodation
  • Add or remove experiences and activities
  • Modify for group size

Just let us know your preferences when you request a quote.

At Virtuoso partner properties (over 2,000 luxury hotels worldwide), you automatically receive:

  • Complimentary daily breakfast for two
  • $100 property credit (spa, dining, or experiences)
  • Room upgrade when available
  • Early check-in and late checkout when available

These perks are included at no extra cost when you book the Best Available Rate.

All payments are processed securely. Typically, we collect a deposit (usually 20-30%) to confirm your booking, with the balance due closer to your travel date.

We accept major credit cards. You still earn your credit card points on all transactions.

You have an advocate. Unlike OTAs where you speak to a different agent every time, we have direct relationships with our partners and can resolve issues quickly.

Flight cancelled? Room not as expected? Need last-minute changes? Contact us and we'll work with the property directly to find a solution.

For Creators

Earning with JUNO

Sign up for early access and connect your Instagram. Share the details of a trip you've taken - the hotels, experiences, and what made it special. We'll work with you to turn it into a bookable itinerary.

You don't need a massive following. You just need to have taken trips worth sharing.

Creators earn 35-55% of the commission on confirmed bookings, depending on tier:

  • Tier 0: $0-$50k TTM GMV = 35%
  • Tier 1: $50k-$300k TTM GMV = 40%
  • Tier 2: $300k-$1m TTM GMV = 45%
  • Tier 3: $1m-$3m TTM GMV = 50%
  • Tier 4: $3m+ TTM GMV = 55%

Commission rates by booking type: Hotels (10-20%), Cruises (10-18%), Tours (15-25%), Car Rentals (8-12%), Travel Insurance (20-30%).

Commission is paid after bookings are completed (post-travel). We handle all invoicing with partners and pay you directly via bank transfer.

You'll have access to an earnings dashboard showing pending and completed commissions.

JUNO handles everything operational:

  • All traveller enquiries and questions
  • Pricing and quote creation
  • Payment processing
  • Booking confirmations and modifications
  • Traveller support before, during, and after trips
  • Commission chasing and invoicing
