The Dhow House

Lamu Island, Kenya

A Private Retreat on the Swahili Coast

The Dhow House is one of Lamu's most coveted private rentals, an exclusive beachfront property that combines traditional Swahili architecture with contemporary luxury. Set on a pristine stretch of sand on Lamu Island, this is where discerning travellers come to truly disconnect.

The house takes its name from the traditional dhow sailing vessels that have plied these waters for centuries. Built using local materials and traditional techniques, the property features hand-carved Lamu doors, coral stone walls, and makuti palm roofing. Yet inside, you'll find every modern comfort: plunge pools, outdoor showers, and spaces designed for both intimate moments and larger gatherings.

What sets The Dhow House apart is its sense of privacy and place. You're steps from the Indian Ocean, yet completely secluded from the world. The house comes fully staffed, including a private chef who prepares fresh seafood and Swahili cuisine daily. Dhow sailing trips, visits to Lamu Old Town, and sunset cruises can all be arranged.

What We Love

  • Absolute privacy — Exclusive use of the entire property
  • Beachfront location — Direct access to pristine sand
  • Swahili architecture — Authentic design with modern luxury
  • Full staff included — Chef, housekeeping, and service team
  • Cultural immersion — Easy access to Lamu Old Town UNESCO site

Best For

The Dhow House is ideal for couples seeking romance and privacy, small groups of friends, or families wanting an exclusive beach retreat. It's particularly special for honeymoons, milestone celebrations, or anyone craving a digital detox in extraordinary surroundings.
