Johannesburg

Four Seasons The Westcliff

Johannesburg's hillside sanctuary. Panoramic views, exceptional service, and the perfect base for exploring Africa's most dynamic city.

Reviewed by Nia The Light

Our Take

The Westcliff has long been Johannesburg's most coveted address. Perched on a cliff in the leafy suburb of Westcliff, it offers something rare in urban hotels: genuine tranquility combined with sweeping views across the city and zoo below. The Four Seasons has elevated this already remarkable property to new heights.

What strikes you first is the sense of space. The grounds are lush and expansive, with terraced gardens that cascade down the hillside. Rooms and suites are generously proportioned, many with private balconies that invite long mornings with coffee and the newspaper.

What We Loved

  • The views — Panoramic vistas across Johannesburg
  • The gardens — Terraced grounds feel like a private estate
  • Flame Restaurant — Outstanding dining with city views
  • The spa — Full-service wellness sanctuary
  • Location — Easy access to galleries and restaurants
  • Service — Four Seasons standard, genuinely warm

The Experience

The Westcliff works equally well as a base for city exploration or a retreat from it. The concierge can arrange gallery visits, township tours, and restaurant reservations. But the property itself invites lingering: breakfast on the terrace, afternoon tea, sunset cocktails with the city lights emerging below.

For safari travelers, The Westcliff is an ideal bookend. Arrive a day early to recover from the long-haul flight, or end your journey here with a few days of art, food, and urban energy before heading home.
