South Africa

A world in one country. Cape Town's beauty, Johannesburg's energy, world-class wine, and safari on your doorstep.

Why South Africa

South Africa is often called "the world in one country" and the description fits. Within its borders you'll find cosmopolitan cities, pristine beaches, dramatic mountains, wine regions rivalling France, and Big Five safari. The infrastructure is excellent, the hospitality world-class, and the diversity of experiences unmatched anywhere on the continent.

For first-time visitors to Africa, South Africa offers an accessible entry point with familiar comforts. For seasoned travellers, it provides depth that rewards repeat visits. Whether you're seeking Cape Town's blend of nature and culture, Johannesburg's art and culinary scenes, or the remote wilderness of the Eastern Cape, South Africa delivers.

The country works beautifully as a standalone destination or as a gateway to the broader region. Many of our itineraries begin or end here, combining urban experiences with safari and beach.

When to Visit

South Africa is a year-round destination, though timing depends on your focus. Cape Town shines from November to March (summer). Safari is best during the dry winter months (May-September). Johannesburg is pleasant year-round. The shoulder seasons offer fewer crowds and good value.

At a Glance

Getting There

Cape Town (CPT) and Johannesburg (JNB) both have excellent international connections. Direct flights from Europe, Middle East, and across Africa.
